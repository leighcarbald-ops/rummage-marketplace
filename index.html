Seller Rules and Responsibilities

Before using Seller Tools, you must read and agree to the following seller rules.

By checking the agreement box and continuing, you understand that selling on this marketplace is a privilege and may be removed at any time by the marketplace administrators.

Seller Booth Fee

1. Sellers must pay a one-time $20.00 booth fee before their first sale post is allowed to be listed on the marketplace.

2. The booth fee must be paid through Cash App to: $S4C2025

3. After payment is made, the seller's first post will remain waiting for approval until an admin confirms the booth fee payment and approves seller access.

4. Once approved, the seller may post items without needing approval for every listing, unless seller access is later revoked by an admin.

5. 100% of the $20.00 booth fee goes to S4C nonprofit.

6. 100% of the money from sold items goes directly to the seller. S4C does not take a percentage of item sales.

7. The booth fee helps support S4C and the operation of the community marketplace.

8. The booth fee is non-refundable unless S4C chooses otherwise.

9. Paying the booth fee does not guarantee permanent seller access. Seller access may be denied, suspended, or revoked for rule violations, unsafe behavior, dishonest listings, or misuse of the marketplace.

Listings

10. Sellers are responsible for making sure all listings are accurate, honest, and complete.

11. Sellers must include clear photos, an accurate item description, item condition, price, and any important details a buyer should know before purchasing.

12. Sellers may not list stolen, illegal, unsafe, counterfeit, recalled, restricted, weapon-related, drug-related, adult, or inappropriate items.

13. Sellers are responsible for removing sold items from the marketplace as soon as possible after sale.

14. Sellers may not intentionally mislead buyers about an item's condition, value, brand, size, working condition, shipping cost, or availability.

Payments

15. All item payments are handled directly between the buyer and seller through the seller's approved outside payment method.

16. The marketplace, S4C, Standing 4 Causes, Stoners 4 a Cause, RunYourAI, admins, organizers, and volunteers do not collect, hold, process, refund, or guarantee seller item payments.

17. Sellers are responsible for resolving payment questions, refunds, disputes, and buyer concerns directly with the buyer.

Shipping

18. Sellers are responsible for figuring out the correct shipping cost before completing the sale.

19. Shipping costs are the buyer's responsibility unless the seller clearly agrees otherwise.

20. Sellers must ship sold items within 4 days of sale unless the buyer and seller both agree to a different timeline.

21. Sellers are responsible for packaging items safely and properly so they are protected during shipping.

22. Sellers must provide the buyer with tracking information after shipping.

23. Sellers must send the buyer a photo of the shipping receipt or tracking receipt as proof that the item was shipped.

Local Pickup

24. If a seller offers local pickup, the seller and buyer are responsible for arranging a safe public meeting location.

25. The marketplace is not responsible for local pickup arrangements, missed meetings, safety issues, or disputes between buyer and seller.

Communication

26. Sellers must communicate with buyers respectfully and honestly.

27. Sellers should respond to buyer questions in a reasonable amount of time.

28. Sellers may not harass, threaten, pressure, scam, or abuse buyers, admins, or other users.

Admin Rights

29. Marketplace admins may remove any listing at any time.

30. Marketplace admins may deny, suspend, or revoke seller access at any time for rule violations, suspicious activity, buyer complaints, unsafe behavior, or misuse of the marketplace.

31. Sellers understand that the marketplace is provided as a community listing platform only. The marketplace does not guarantee sales, payments, shipping, item quality, buyer behavior, or seller success.
